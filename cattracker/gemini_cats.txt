NAMES: Peaches, Pancake, Snoop, Socks

Use the following descriptions to identify these cats in CCTV images. There are four distinct cats in this household:

Peaches (calico): look for a tricolor coat with a white base and distinct patches of orange and black.

Pancake (brown tabby): identified by bold, high-contrast dark brown/black "mackerel" stripes on a tan or warm brown background — you should see stripe edges or a banded pattern, not a single flat color. large pointed ears and green eyes are typical. This is NOT Snoop: any visible tabby striping means Pancake (or unknown), never the solid black cat.

Snoop (black cat / void): ONLY if the body fur reads as one solid dark color with NO tabby stripes and NO warm brown undertone on the coat. no white markings. large round "owl-like" yellow or light green eyes against black fur. If the cat might be a dark brown tabby in poor light, choose Pancake or unknown — do not default to Snoop.

Socks (tuxedo/bicolor): a black and white cat. features a black "mask" over the eyes and ears, a white chest and neck, and a small, distinct black smudge or "beauty mark" on the chin.
